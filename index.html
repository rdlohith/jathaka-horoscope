Method & authenticity

Why this jāthaka is correct

A horoscope is only as true as the sky it is drawn from. This kundali is built on two foundations at once - the exact astronomy of the heavens, and the classical rules of Vedic jyotiṣa - so the numbers are astronomically real and the framework is shastra-sammat (scripture-consistent).

Astronomically exact

Every planetary longitude comes from the same ephemeris model used by astronomers and space agencies - not an approximation.

  • Swiss-Ephemeris-grade positions. Sun, Moon and planets are computed to arc-second precision from the astronomy-engine ephemeris, entirely on your device. Validated arc-minute against pyswisseph (the Swiss Ephemeris) on real charts - every sign, degree, nakṣatra and pada matched.
  • Lahiri ayanāṁśa, exact. The precession correction to the sidereal zodiac reproduces the Swiss Ephemeris to under 0.001 arc-second across 1900-2100.
  • Mean-node Rāhu & Ketu. The lunar nodes are the true astronomical points where the Moon's path crosses the ecliptic.
  • No internet, no AI, no guesswork. The mathematics is deterministic - the same inputs always yield the same chart, and nothing is ever invented.

Traditionally authentic

The interpretive framework follows the classical texts - Bṛhat Parāśara Horā Śāstra and Jaimini - as practised for centuries.

  • Sidereal (Nirayaṇa) zodiac. Uses the fixed-star zodiac with the Lahiri (Chitrapakṣa) ayanāṁśa - the Government of India's Rāṣṭrīya Pañcāṅga standard, not the Western tropical zodiac.
  • 27 Nakṣatras & Vimśottari Daśā. The Moon's nakṣatra sets the 120-year daśā timeline exactly as the śāstra prescribes.
  • Whole-sign Bhāvas, Parāśari & Jaimini. Houses, aspects (dṛṣṭi), yogas, kārakas, Ashtakavarga, Shadbala, vargas and doshas all follow the classical rules.
  • Full Pañcāṅga. Tithi, Vāra, Nakṣatra, Yoga and Karaṇa are computed traditionally - the same five limbs a purohit reads. Śrī Gaṇeśāya Namaḥ 🕉
Why the time matters
The Lagna (ascendant) moves about 1° every 4 minutes - a whole sign every ~2 hours. An accurate birth time is what fixes the rising sign, the house framework and every divisional chart.
4 min ≈ 1° of Lagna
Why the longitude matters
Your east-west position sets the Local Sidereal Time - which part of the zodiac is on the eastern horizon at your exact place and moment. A different longitude means a different Lagna degree.
sets local sidereal time
Why the latitude matters
Your north-south position sets the angle at which the ecliptic meets the horizon, shaping the ascendant and the house cusps. The timezone converts your clock time to Universal Time for the ephemeris.
